01 — The Filmmaker

About

Raiym Bilal is a filmmaker with seven years of experience across film, commercial, and advertising productions. A graduate of Fanshawe College and The Millennium Universal College, he has developed a strong foundation in filmmaking through both formal education and hands-on industry work.

Over the years, he has contributed to dozens of productions, collaborating with multinational studios and production teams across commercial campaigns, branded content, and independent films. His experience spans production assistance, on-set coordination, cinematography support, and close collaboration with art and prop departments.

Driven by a passion for visual storytelling, Raiym approaches every project with a focus on strong narrative, cinematic composition, and meaningful collaboration.
